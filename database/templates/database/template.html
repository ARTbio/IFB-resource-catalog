Catalog of French resources in bioinformatics

Catalogue Description

IFB will ensure the visibility and accessibility of bioinformatics resources (tools, databases, individual expertises, platforms and training material), which will be synchronized with related international catalogues.
This catalogue will also contribute to readily identify the most adapted resources and their availability to support end-user needs

This catalogue will encompass different categories of entities that can be considered as bioinformatics resources.

  • Software tools.
  • Databases.
  • Organizations (we will consider any type of structure contributing to research or support in bioinformatics: platforms of services, research laboratories, SME, ...).
  • Persons and their expertise (“Who knows what?”).
  • Training events and material.
    • It will include an inventory of the locations where each resource can be found (platforms, servers, ...).{% endblock %}
